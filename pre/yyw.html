The challenge

Why Freshman Enquiries Are Difficult

The difficulty is not just understanding a question. Nova must locate the right campus information and return it without inventing an answer.

01

Information is scattered

Notices, handbooks, orientation materials, emails, and online platforms divide one student journey across many sources.

The answer may exist — but the student may not know where.

02

Questions are informal

Freshmen use short, incomplete, and varied wording for the same issue. The system must understand meaning beyond exact phrasing.

“Moodle cannot enter” and “learning platform login problem” may mean the same thing.

Key motivation03

Answers must be verified

Campus questions often involve procedures or rules. Answers must be grounded in verified FAQ data, not plausible guesses.

When evidence is weak, Nova should clarify or fall back — not guess.

Useful answers require both language understanding and reliable evidence.

Design rationale

Why Hybrid?

No single method covers intent handling, question understanding, evidence retrieval, and uncertainty equally well.

Single-method chatbot problems

×Pure rule-based systems are rigid
×Pure ML classification does not answer by itself
×Free-form generation may produce unsupported answers
×Pure keyword search misses varied wording

strengths

Nova hybrid solution

Hybrid design makes Nova more reliable, explainable, and suitable for campus FAQ answering.
