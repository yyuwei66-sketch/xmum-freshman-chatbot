05 · Practical choice

Conclusion and Insights

01

Linear SVM achieved the best held-out test performance: 0.7680 accuracy and 0.7186 macro F1.

02

Campus life and Academic Affairs performed strongly.

03

Campus Services and IT Support were harder, likely due to fewer samples and overlapping wording.

04

Next steps: add balanced data, tune parameters, and improve category-specific examples.
